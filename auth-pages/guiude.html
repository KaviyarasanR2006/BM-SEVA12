Biodegradable Waste

Organic waste that decomposes naturally into the environment.

  • Food leftovers
  • Vegetable peels
  • Garden leaves
  • Paper, wood

Non-Biodegradable Waste

Waste that does not decompose easily and needs recycling.

  • Plastics, Glass
  • Metals, Cans
  • Rubber products

Hazardous Waste

Waste that is harmful to health and needs special handling.

  • Batteries, Chemicals
  • Medical waste
  • Expired medicines

Color Bins Explained

Green Bin Biodegradable waste (Food, leaves, compost)
Blue Bin Dry recyclable waste (Paper, plastics, metals)
Red Bin Sanitary waste (Diapers, napkins)
Yellow Bin E-waste and chemical waste
Black Bin Reject waste (Glass, ceramics, dust)
