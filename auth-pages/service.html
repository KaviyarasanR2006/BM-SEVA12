Biowaste Clearance

A civic reporting system where citizens can capture and report issues like waste dumping or drainage problems through an application. The system automatically forwards the report, including photos and location, to the municipal authorities, ensuring prompt resolution and fostering a cleaner environment.

Emergency Service

Emergency biowaste clearance refers to the rapid and safe removal of biological waste in situations that pose immediate risks to public health, safety, or the environment. This is particularly critical in cases of medical waste overflow, biohazard spills, or natural disasters.
